Welcome to Phoenix ENT.

Phoenix ENT. is a tours and travels company situated in Hurlingham, Nairobi. We are committed in offering the ideal vacation package for all who want to take vacations and trips in Nairobi and Kenya at large. Currently we only have offices in Nairobi but it is our plan to open other branches in the United Kingdom, Saudi Arabia, The United States of America and Norway.
