Phoenix ENT. is a tours and travels company situated in Hurlingham, Nairobi. We are committed in offering the ideal vacation package for all who want to take vacations and trips in Nairobi and Kenya at large.
Currently we only have offices in Nairobi but it is our plan to open other branches in the United Kingdom, Saudi Arabia, The United States of America and Norway.

Our Team

At Phoenix ENT, we have a dedicated team of travel enthusiasts who are passionate about creating exceptional experiences for our clients. Each member of our team brings a wealth of knowledge and expertise to ensure that your journey with us is nothing short of extraordinary.

About Us

Welcome to Phoenix ENT, your gateway to unforgettable travel experiences. We are a premier tours and travel company dedicated to providing exceptional services and creating memorable adventures for our clients.

With a passion for exploration and a commitment to excellence, we curate a wide range of tour packages and itineraries that cater to various interests and preferences. Whether you're seeking a relaxing beach getaway, an adrenaline-pumping adventure, or a culturally enriching journey, we have the perfect trip for you.

At Phoenix ENT, we understand that every traveler is unique. Our team of experienced travel experts is dedicated to personalizing your travel experience, ensuring that each trip is tailored to your specific needs and desires. From handpicked accommodations to carefully crafted activities, we strive to exceed your expectations and make your dream vacation a reality.

With our extensive network of trusted partners and local guides, we take pride in offering immersive travel experiences that showcase the beauty, diversity, and hidden gems of each destination. We prioritize sustainability and responsible tourism, working closely with local communities to preserve the environment and support local economies.

Join us on an extraordinary journey and let Phoenix ENT be your companion in discovering the world's wonders. Whether you're a solo traveler, a couple, or a group of adventurers, we are here to make your travel dreams come true. Contact us today and let's embark on an unforgettable adventure together!
